In the field of Visual Arts with around five years so far, Design; mostly editorial and corporate, Photography and Illustration, the disciplines I feel keener to in this field.

Going through the brief in play. Objectives, time frame, budget; in order to analyze my boundaries then I can visualize all the possible solutions and proceed with the brainstorm, mood board, etc. If there is any background material, antecessors to the project and your position about it (requirements/reference if any). We’ll move on to the proof, corrections and finally production.

In short if I may; once received the assignment, I traduce the need, check antecedents and analyze impact of previous solution if any, brainstorming, plan execution, art work and proof to lastly execute.

In regards to the goals achieved to this point I can mention; in the given case created and maintained the graphic imagery for the materials and other marketing outreach. Covering from brainstorming to the production. Designed new on-brand visual elements to effectively convey concepts and messaging.

For each project I look forward to be creative and practical to put it as an added value to my work.

Added to that also landed/simplified extended complex concept which can be in the daily soup for any graphic designer. In my case, I look at the big picture and then go for the relevant details.
